Color.

Privacy Policy

Effective: May 25, 2026

The Short Version

Color. does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device. There are no servers, no accounts, no analytics, and no ads.

Developer

Color. is published by Pears, a sole proprietorship operated by Marino Solokhov, based in Germany.

Data Collection

Color. collects no data. Specifically:

  • No personal information is collected
  • No usage data or analytics are collected
  • No data is transmitted over the internet
  • No cookies or tracking technologies are used
  • No third-party SDKs or services are integrated
  • No advertising frameworks are included
  • No accounts or registration are required

Device Permissions

Color. requests access to the following device capabilities:

  • Camera: Used to capture live frames for real-time color analysis. Camera data is processed entirely on-device and is never stored, transmitted, or shared.
  • Photo Library: Used to import photos for palette extraction. Images are processed locally and never leave your device.

Both permissions are optional. The app functions without them (Studio and Favorites tabs do not require any permissions).

Data Storage

All user-generated content (favorite colors, palettes, preferences) is stored locally on your device using Apple's standard persistence frameworks (SwiftData and UserDefaults). This data is included in your iCloud backup if you have device backups enabled, but is never accessible to the developer.

Third-Party Services

Color. uses no third-party services, SDKs, APIs, or frameworks beyond Apple's standard iOS SDK. The app makes zero network requests.

Children's Privacy

Color. does not collect data from anyone, including children under 13 (or the applicable age in your jurisdiction). The app is safe for use by all ages.

Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted here with a new effective date. Since the app collects no data, meaningful changes are unlikely.

Contact

Questions about this privacy policy can be directed to:
privacy@pears.app

Your Rights Under GDPR

Under the European General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), you have the right to access, rectify, delete, and port your personal data. Since Color. collects no personal data, there is nothing to access, rectify, delete, or port. If you have questions about your rights, contact us at the address above.
